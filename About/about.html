HYUN IS AN EXPERIENCE DESIGNER

Currently pursuing MHCI at Carnegie Mellon University

I'm an experience designer, interested in creating physical and digital experience through user-centered design. I have range of experience from international corporate projects to start-up development, as well as in-depth user research and design strategy.

As an experience designer, my goal is to design and build experience that is relevant and meaningful whether it is physical or digital. A better experience starts with understanding problem, constraints, and objectives and ends with design derived from solid reasoning with creativity. I care about the details and consider the user-centered process to be crucial in creating intuitive yet effective design.

I am currently based in Pittsburgh, pursuing Master of Human Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University. Please feel free to contact me!
